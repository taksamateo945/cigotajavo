There are Some Crazy Dylan Sprouse Fanpages on Tumblr

Former child star, Dylan Sprouse, is most popular for starring in the Disney Channel series, The Suite Life of Zack and Cody, and its spin-off, The Suite Life on Deck.  In the Suit Life of Zack and Cody, Dylan played the role of Zack Martin, a young boy who lived in the fictional Tipton Hotel with his on-screen and real life twin brother, Cody Martin (Cole Sprouse).  The show aired from 2005-2008 and earned Dylan several awards, including the 2009 Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Award for Favorite TV Actor.  During their tenure on the show, Dylan and Cole were among the richest child actors alive.  When the Suite Life of Zack and Cody ended in 2008, the show transitioned into a spiff of series called The Suit Life on Deck, which featured the characters living on a cruise ship called the SS Tipton instead of in The Tipton hotel.  The show earned Dylan and his brother  $40,000 per episode, making them the highest paid television stars on the Disney Channel.  During the show’s run, Dylan reprised his role as Zack and made guest appearances on several other Disney Channel series including That’s So Raven and The Wizards of Waverly Place.

The series eventually spawned a Disney Channel original movie called, The Suite Life Movie, which recorded more than five million viewers on its original air date.  In 2011, The Suite Life on Deck docked for good and Dylan decided to take a break from the spotlight to attend college at New York University where he is currently studying video game design.  In recent years, Dylan has been largely absent from the acting world, and last year he announced that he was working  as a server on a New York City restaurant. However, Dylan Sprouce’s fans certainly haven’t forgotten about him and the star-studded actor he once was.  Many of Dylan Sprouse’s fans have used the popular blogging cite, Tumblr, to create elaborate fan pages expressing their love.  While some of the pages are no longer being frequently updated, they all serve as a tribute to one of the guys who showed the world how to enjoy the suite life.  Here are the best Dylan Sprouse fan pages on Tumblr.

I love Dylan Sprouse, Miley Cyrus and Selena Gomez <3″ The Fatmanur page, also known as, Dylan Sprouse Love, wastes no time showing just how dedicated its members are.  As soon as viewers enter the site they are greeted with a definition of what it means to be a fan that proves exactly why the word is short for fanatic: ”

Apparently, these are just the kind of fans that Dylan Sprouse attracts.

The S3xyDylan (yes, that is a three instead of an e) Tumblr page is dedicated to Dylan Sprouse and proudly declares “Dylan Sprouse is sexy” on its main page.  The blog, which features a background made entirely of McDonald’s Big Macs, currently has 102 pages of Sprouse related content including screenshots, GIFs, and memes.  The page also has numerous selfies taken by fan across the world who were lucky enough to have a real-life run in with Dylan

For those of you who may have been wondering, “This is not the real Dylan and Cole Sprouse but only a fan made blog.”  Now that that’s cleared up, visitors are free to enjoy the many pictures and GIFs that depict Dylan and his brother throughout their careers.  The page also features the song “Welcome to the Tipton” that was originally served as a commercial for The Suit Life of Zack and Cody’s fictitious hotel.

This page isn’t just for people who are fans of Dylan Sprouse; it is for people who are addicted to him.  While the blog also includes pictures and GIFs of other celebrities, the blog’s creator makes it clear that her loyalties lie when Dylan Sprouse and One Direction.  Although the site contains mostly images, it also features a statement that was supposedly written by Dylan addressing all of the criticism he received after he decided to shave his hair into a mohawk.  Many of Dylan’s face did not find the hairstyle flattering and did not hesitate to tell him; however, Dylan remained calm and in his statement he made sure to politely let people know their opinions don’t phase him.  After rehashing some of the conversations he had after he cut his hair, Dylan said: “Hair is hair. It grows back. Mine grows back particularly fast. Cole and I have always had odd hairstyles to say the least. This is historically accurate lol. Not only that, but hair is very much an expression of yourself. Keyword expression. I implore everyone to experiment with their hair, as not only is it fun, but it is liberating in many ways to stray away from the social norm.”

This page is sure to announce that only “Dylan Sprouse lovers” are admitted.  Although this secret Sprouse society has been fairly inactive for a while, it’s content is certainly centered around Dylan and his many acting roles.  This page is filled with images of Dylan and Cole, and thanks to sites like this, the young stars will always be able to look back at some of the best moments during their Suite Life days and era.
