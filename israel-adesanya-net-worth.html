Israel Adesanya Net Worth

Israel Adesanya, the former Middleweight champion, is a highly recognized figure in the UFC.

Despite accusations of underpaying athletes, Adesanya considers himself an exception to this. Recent reports show that the Nigerian-New Zealander has a net worth of approximately $6M.

Who Is Israel Adesanya?

Israel Mobolaji Odunayo Oluwafemi Temitayo Owolabi Adesanya was born on July 22, 1989, in Lagos, Nigeria. As a child, he split his time between Nigeria and New Zealand.

During his childhood, Adesanya did not participate in athletics and was bullied by his peers as a result.

It wasn’t until his college years that Adesanya began training in kickboxing. He won all 32 of his amateur matches and decided to turn professional, heading to China.

To improve his skills, Adesanya started training in boxing and wrestling. After years of practicing both boxing and kickboxing, he made his professional mixed martial arts debut in 2012.

Israel Adesanya Net Worth And Career

Israel Adesanya won his first 11 professional matches, including the AFC Middleweight Championship and the Hex Fight Series Middleweight Championship.

He made his UFC debut at UFC 221, defeating Rob Wilkinson via technical knockout and earning himself the Performance of the Night bonus.

Adesanya continued to win and received the Performance of the Night several more times.

He defeated Anderson Silva, Brad Tavares, and others on his way to his first championship bout.

At UFC 236, he faced Kelvin Gastelum for the interim UFC Middleweight Championship and won via unanimous decision.

Over the next few years, Adesanya successfully defended his title five times and even contended for the UFC Light Heavyweight Championship but lost to Jan Blachowicz.

In November 2022, Adesanya lost the UFC Middleweight Championship due to a technical knockout against Alex Pereira.

Israel Adesanya Net Worth and Career Earnings

Before joining the UFC, Israel Adesanya earned modest payouts from his previous promotions. However, he had already started building his net worth by then.

Once he signed up with the UFC, Adesanya’s earnings increased substantially.

He made around half a million dollars during his first year in the company, which included four fights that paid him six-figure checks.

Adesanya’s salary received a significant boost after his fight with Robert Whittaker at UFC 243, where he earned $1 million.

In recent years, Adesanya has earned seven-figure payouts from fights against renowned fighters like Paulo Costa, Jan Blachowicz, and Marvin Vettori.

Adesanya’s continued success has made him incredibly wealthy, and sources estimate that the former UFC Middleweight Champion is worth approximately $6 million.

Personal Life

Israel Adesanya grew up with his four siblings under the care of his parents Oluwafemi and Taiwo Adesanya.

During his childhood, he spent time living in Nigeria, Ghana, and New Zealand.

Although Adesanya has not tied the knot yet and doesn’t have any children, he has been linked to some notable personalities in the past.

He was once romantically associated with influencer Shana Evers and actress Margot Robbie.

Currently, he is believed to be in a relationship with Charlotte Powdrell from Auckland, New Zealand.

Adesanya maintains an official website and is quite active on social media. He regularly posts updates on his official Twitter and Instagram accounts.

Israel Adesanya’s Girlfriend

Adesanya had never openly discussed his relationships, but he dropped a hint to his observant fans by sharing pictures of himself with a woman.

His fans later discovered that the lady in the photos was none other than the well-known Australian model, Shana Evers.

Israel Adesanya has earned numerous awards throughout his career in mixed martial arts, boxing, and kickboxing.

He has been recognized as one of the world’s best fighters due to his impressive track record in various competitions.

  • In 2016, Adesanya won the Glory Middleweight Contender Tournament, and he has also won three championships at King in the Ring in the 86-kilogram category.
  • Adesanya has won two Cruiserweight Championships with Super 8 Boxing, and he has received several Performance of the Night and Fight of the Night bonuses in the UFC.

Israel Adesanya’s Endorsements

‘The Last Stylebender’ earns through endorsements apart from his UFC earnings.

He has notable brand endorsements such as being the first MMA star to have Puma as a sponsor.

He is also an Ambassador for Stake.com and has partnered with ‘Call of Duty’ and ‘Engage’ clothing for limited edition collections.
