Jane Levy From Suburgatory Getting Divorced, Was Secretly Married to Jaime Freitas

Jane Levy's secret is out! The Suburgatory actress filed for divorce from actor Jaime Freitas on Apr. 16, TMZ reports. Levy, who has never mentioned being married in interviews, cited "irreconcilable differences" as the cause of their split.

The 23-year-old redhead, whose film credits include Evil Dead and Fun-Size, got married in March 2011. Levy and Freitas separated seven months later, according to court documents. The actress indicated she does not want to pay spousal support, TMZ adds.
