Mateo Salvatto Wikipedia And Career

Mateo Salvatto, a young tech entrepreneur and innovator, was born in 1999 in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

From a young age, Salvatto demonstrated a deep passion for technology and a desire to make a difference in the world. He attended ORT Argentina, a school specializing in electronics, where he honed his skills and embarked on various exciting projects.

During his time at ORT Argentina, Salvatto and his partner achieved international recognition as the champions of the “Robotraffic” competition at the Technion Institute of Technology in Haifa, Israel, in 2016.

This early success fueled Salvatto’s ambition to create innovative solutions that could positively impact people’s lives.

In pursuit of his goal, Salvatto developed an application called ‘Hablalo’ with the assistance of individuals with hearing disabilities.

Hablalo is a real-time speech-to-text translation app that facilitates communication for individuals who are deaf or hard of hearing.

Then, Salvatto’s app quickly gained popularity, garnering 62,000 active users from various regions worldwide, including Latin America, Europe, Asia, Africa, and North America, within just two years.

Motivated by the success of Hablalo and his desire to help more people, Salvatto co-founded Asteroid Technologies, a tech start-up dedicated to developing and supporting free life-changing products for individuals in vulnerable situations.

Salvatto’s commitment to making a positive impact through technology has garnered him numerous accolades and recognition from prestigious institutions.

Similarly, he has been named the Humanitarian Innovator of the Year by MIT Technology Review and the Best Social Innovator Worldwide by Peking University, among other notable awards.

Today, Salvatto serves as the CEO of Asteroid Technologies. The comapny boasts a client roster that includes banks, real estate companies, and governments.

Additionally, he holds the position of Head of Innovation for ORT Technical Schools in Buenos Aires, where he continues to inspire and mentor young minds in the technology field.

With his innovative solutions and entrepreneurial spirit, Salvatto has become a prominent figure in the tech industry, positively impacting the lives of individuals with disabilities and advocating for inclusion worldwide.
