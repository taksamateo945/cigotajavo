Salma Hayek Shows Off Her Beach Body In Plunging Bathing Suit After Sharing Throw Back Swimsuit Phot

Salma Hayek is still just as gorgeous as ever and she has the photos to prove it! Sharing several pictures on her official Instagram account, the actress posted a throwback photo where she wore an H&M bikini for a 1998 campaign and then followed up the photos with recent snaps of herself wearing a one-piece bathing suit that featured a plunging neckline. Salma’s fans couldn’t believe how she is just as gorgeous now as she was in the original photoshoot. Salma just celebrated her 54th birthday on September 2, 2020, but you would never know it by looking at her. Some say Salma even looks better now than she did then!

Salma shared two photos in the slideshow. The first pictured the Mexican-born actress posing on her side in the burgundy-colored swimwear. Even in those pictures, she is age-defying. The photos were taken when she was 33-years-old but she looks like she is in her twenties!
