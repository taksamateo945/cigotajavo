Saundra Powell Obituary (1940 - 2022) - Bountiful, UT

Saundra Josephson Powell passed away on May 16, 2022. Saundra was born on June 27, 1940 in Salt Lake City Utah, to Della Bradley Jacobsen and Joseph Abraham Josephson. She married Ralph Ellis Powell on December 27, 1954 and together they had 3 wonderful children, Ronald Powell, Debra Webb (Allen), and Tom Powell (Laura). Saundra is survived by her 3 children, several grandchildren and great grandchildren. Saundra was preceded in death by her parents, husband, brother and sisters.

Saundra worked as a florist for many years, and enjoyed arranging flowers. She loved animals, especially cats, and donated to many animal charities. Saundra loved all cats, unless that cat would kill a bird and drop it on her front porch. Saundra also loved reading the daily obituaries from the newspaper, and saved ones she found funny.

Saundra will always be remembered for the love she had for her family, especially her grandchildren. Saundra had a great many photographs of her grandchildren all over her house, and she would talk to anyone who would listen about how proud she was of them. Saundra also meticulously cared for her house and yard. She took great pride in making sure her home looked nice, and was especially proud of the dozens of rooster figurines that she collected and displayed.

The family would like to thank Elevation Hospice, and the many nurses and staff at Life Care Center in Bountiful, for caring for, and loving our mom.

Family and friends are invited to a graveside services on Saturday, May 21, 2022, at 11:00 am at the Elysian Burial Gardens, 1075 East 4580 South, Millcreek, Utah.
