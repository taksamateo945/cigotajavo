Ward Thomas Release 'Boomerang' Video

Record making country music duo Ward Thomas yesterday announced that the official music video for their brand new single ‘Boomerang’ would be getting released “soon”. 

Boomerang video coming soon!!! ? pic.twitter.com/j41vE3PQIR

— Ward Thomas (@wardthomasmusic) January 22, 2017

And fans of the 22-year-old twin sisters Catherine and Lizzy, didn’t have to wait long for the video to premier.

Less than 24-hours later Ward Thomas had officially unveiled the clip. The music video for the toe-tapping brilliant ‘Boomerang’ is a live performance of the track which was filmed at the Roundhouse in London in December of last year.

Here it is! Filmed at @RoundhouseLDN #Boomerang! Watch the full video here https://t.co/wMjDwEJ0vJ ? pic.twitter.com/57CboK1TLN

— Ward Thomas (@wardthomasmusic) January 23, 2017

The single is the fourth track to be lifted from their record breaking number 1 album ‘Cartwheels‘, and is also their fourth consecutive BBC Radio 2 A Listed song.

You can watch the official music video for the wonderful ‘Boomerang’ in full here:

‘Boomerang’ is officially released on the 3rd of February.

Tickets for Ward Thomas’ Spring 2017 tour are on sale now.
